# Rubric: tone contrast (v1)

Dimension: **tone**

Two replies answer the identical customer prompt. One was generated with a
**warm** tone directive, the other with a **professional** tone directive.

PASS only if the two replies differ in a way a human reader would attribute to
tone, in the expected direction:

- The **warm** variant reads as more empathetic, personable, and approachable
  (e.g. acknowledges feelings, uses friendlier phrasing) without being
  saccharine or over-apologetic.
- The **professional** variant reads as more polished, direct, and businesslike
  without being cold, legalistic, or robotic.

FAIL if the two replies are effectively interchangeable in tone, if the
difference goes the wrong direction, or if either reply is off-topic or fails to
address the prompt.

Ignore differences in factual content, length, and formatting — judge tone only.
