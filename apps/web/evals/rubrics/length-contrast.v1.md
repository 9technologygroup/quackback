# Rubric: response-length contrast (v1)

Dimension: **length**

Two replies answer the identical customer prompt. One was generated with a
**brief** response-length directive, the other with a **detailed** directive.

PASS only if:

- The **brief** variant is noticeably shorter and more compact — the shortest
  complete answer, typically one short paragraph or a compact list.
- The **detailed** variant is meaningfully longer, with fuller explanation
  and/or ordered steps, while staying on topic.

The detailed reply should be substantially longer than the brief one (a rough
guide: at least ~1.5x the length), and the difference should read as
intentional thoroughness, not padding.

FAIL if the lengths are comparable, if the "brief" reply is longer than the
"detailed" one, or if either reply fails to address the prompt.
