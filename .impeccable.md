## Design Context

### Users

Quackback serves product and support teams collecting, triaging, prioritizing, and closing the loop on customer feedback. The admin interface primarily supports daily power users moving frequently between feedback, support, publishing, analytics, automation, user, and configuration surfaces.

### Brand Personality

Efficient, calm, and capable. The product should feel focused and approachable without sacrificing the density expected from an operational tool.

### Aesthetic Direction

Use a refined utilitarian product aesthetic that preserves Quackback's established visual language in light and dark themes. Favor compact navigation, clear state changes, restrained color, and familiar controls over decorative UI.

### Design Principles

- Optimize admin workflows for repeated daily use and fast navigation.
- Keep related controls compact while preserving clear focus, hover, and active states.
- Use the existing component primitives, typography, color tokens, and spacing scale.
- Preserve comfortable touch targets on mobile; apply higher density where pointer input is expected.
- Prefer quiet hierarchy and purposeful spacing over decorative containers or effects.
